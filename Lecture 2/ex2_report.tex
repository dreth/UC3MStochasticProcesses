First we solve \(\pi P = \pi\):

\(\pi (P-I) = (1,0, \dots, 0)\)

\((\pi_{1}, \pi_{2}, \pi_{3}) \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0.4 & 0.5 \\ 1 & -0.4 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & -0.6 \end{pmatrix} = (1,0,0)\)

Solving the system we get:

\(\pi = (\frac{6}{17}, \frac{15}{17}, \frac{10}{17})\)

Which is our stationary distribution.
